This project is a complete Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard developed to analyze telecom customer behavior and identify key factors contributing to customer churn. The objective is to help organizations understand risk segments and improve customer retention strategies.

Project Overview

Total customers: 7,043

Churned customers: 1,869

Overall churn rate: 26.5%

Tool used: Microsoft Power BI

Key Insights

Month-to-month contract customers show the highest churn.

Customers using the electronic check payment method have the highest churn rate.

Fiber optic internet service users churn more compared to DSL customers.

Customers with low tenure (less than 12 months) are more likely to churn.

Customers without partners or dependents tend to churn more frequently.

Conclusion

This dashboard provides a clear understanding of customer churn trends and highlights the major factors influencing customer dropout. The insights can assist businesses in identifying at-risk customers and implementing data-driven retention strategies.
